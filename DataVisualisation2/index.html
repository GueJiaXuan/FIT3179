Fact 1:

NBA Players and their Nationalities

At the start of the 2019-2020 NBA season, there are a total of 429 players, coming to play the game of basketball from 38 different countries. The NBA is played in the United States of America. Even though the NBA is a sport where the entire world population enjoys watching, only 38 countries have players that are part of the NBA out of the 195 countries present in this world. That is only 19.4% of the countries.

Out of the 429 NBA players, 323 playes are Americans, which take up 71.9% of the entire NBA players population. The donut chart below will showcase the nationality of the NBA players.

Visualising NBA Player's Nationality with a Donut Chart

As clearly seen, the Americans take up a large majority, followed by the Canadians, and then by the Australians. Diversity is not really present in the NBA. This could be due to many reason such that other country have their own basketball leagues, or that team scouting are not willing to travel around the world to find the best basketball players as that may be too much of an effort.

Fact 2:

NBA Teams and the number of players in each team

The NBA consists of 30 teams. Since this dataset is created at the start of the NBA season, there are players still not picked by a team yet. The NBA limits each team to at least 15 players during the regular season, but teams are allowed to have up to 20 players.
